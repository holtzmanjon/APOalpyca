lts150 module
*************

Low level module to interface with Thorlabs stage controller. 

In addition to operational methods, class also provides attributes
and methods as required by the `ASCOM Alpaca Focuser device <https://ascom-standards.org/alpyca/alpaca.focuser.html>`_.
